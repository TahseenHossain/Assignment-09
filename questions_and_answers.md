```javascript

console.log(food);
```

- B: `['🍝', '🍫', '🥑', '🍔']`
- C: `['🍝', '🍕', '🍫', '🥑', '🍔']`
- D: `ReferenceError`

<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>

#### Answer: A

<i>We have copied data from the food array and put it in the info object. Than we changed the value of info with "🍝". But this did not changed the value of the food array. So output will be A/i>

</p>
</details>
